# Roll-Rite Strategy Deck Conveyor Pilot

Source deck:

- `G:\My Drive\BPM Software Solutions\Clients\Roll-Rite\Executive Summary\RR Growth Roadmap Final 121317.pptx`

Observed deck shape:

- 47 slides
- Strategy-oriented rather than product-spec oriented
- Strong spine around Vision 2022, growth criteria, strategic priorities, 5x5 growth strategy, and five major initiatives

Extracted strategy spine:

1. Expand service and install centers.
2. Grow for-hire and private-fleet sales and services.
3. Upsell and upgrade existing Roll-Rite tarps.
4. Acquire, make, and sell retractable flatbed tarps.
5. Push international expansion.

What the conveyor can reasonably project from this deck:

1. A growth roadmap app that turns strategy themes into execution workstreams.
2. A fleet targeting and account-planning workspace for for-hire and private-fleet sales.
3. A service-center expansion planner with geography, demand, and rollout stages.
4. A value-selling cockpit for tarp upgrades, replacement cycles, and attach-rate opportunities.
5. An acquisition diligence workspace for retractable flatbed tarp expansion.
6. An international expansion cockpit with market sequencing and release gates.
7. A strategy-to-execution dashboard that ties initiatives to owners, milestones, and receipts.

Why this is a good pilot:

- The source is already structured as a business compiler input, not just a slideshow.
- The deck has a clear decision spine, so the extraction step can stay faithful instead of inventing business meaning.
- The initiatives are concrete enough to become product surfaces without needing a lot of speculative interpretation.

Conveyor path for this pilot:

```text
PPTX strategy deck
-> source capture
-> story extraction
-> canonical strategy spec
-> initiative ontology
-> product candidate mapping
-> projection surface selection
-> executable artifact or doc projection
-> evidence receipt
```

What we still need for a full live implementation:

- A deck ingestion lane that emits slide text, structure, and source receipts.
- A strategy ontology that can distinguish themes, initiatives, KPIs, markets, and constraints.
- A product-mapping contract that can turn initiative clusters into app candidates.
- A projection registry for document, dashboard, and presentation surfaces.
- A receipt bridge so the source deck, extracted meaning, and downstream projection all stay line-linked.

Assessment:

- This deck is suitable for a document-to-product pilot.
- It is not yet itself a runnable product specification.
- The conveyor can already use it as source material for a governed extraction and projection flow.

Recommended next step:

- Run one narrow pilot slice that turns the Roll-Rite deck into a canonical strategy spec plus a small execution dashboard projection.
