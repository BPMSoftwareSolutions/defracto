# Body Disposition

Every body must know its place in the story.

No body without lineage.
No body without disposition.
No body without purpose.

Body disposition is the discipline for classifying bounded meaning-bearing artifacts before they become hand-authored executable behavior.

## Body Layers

- Narrative taxonomy
- Semantic taxonomy
- Body taxonomy
- Disposition taxonomy
- Execution taxonomy
- Evidence taxonomy
- Drift taxonomy

## Seed Principle

A body can be detected, classified, and given a disposition before any hand-authored executable body exists.

That classification keeps future execution from becoming the source of truth.
